Creation & Developement

Spider-Man is a superhero created by Marvel Comics who gains arachnid-like powers, such as sticking to surfaces and super-human agility, after being bitten by a radioactive spider. The character uses these amazing abilities to fight crime and help others, but often struggles with a complicated personal life. The main human identity of Spider-Man is Peter Parker, a young man whose experiences growing up and managing his dual identity have provided endless challenges for the character
